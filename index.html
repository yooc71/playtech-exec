25 Years of History

From Tartu to the
World Stage

Every major milestone that shaped the most comprehensive gaming technology company on earth.

1999
Founded in Tartu, Estonia

Teddy Sagi founds Playtech with a vision to build the most advanced online gaming software. First client signs within 12 months.

2006
IPO on London Stock Exchange

Playtech lists on the LSE, raising £117.5M. Shares surge 40% on debut — one of the largest tech IPOs that year.

2011
William Hill Partnership

Landmark deal with William Hill — cementing Playtech's status as the premier B2B platform for Tier 1 UK operators.

2016
IMS Platform Launched

Information Management System — a single omni-channel player account management system — becomes the industry benchmark for player data intelligence.

2018
Snaitech Acquisition — €846M

Bold move into B2C: Playtech acquires Italy's leading gambling group, gaining 1,800+ physical betting shops and a dominant digital brand.

2021

2022–24
US Expansion Begins

Strategic technology partnerships with Hard Rock Digital and BetMGM. Playtech enters the world's most valuable emerging gaming market as a primary technology partner.

2026
